Genuine cohesion

Truthful, dignity-preserving, agency-respecting relation. Repair after rupture, reciprocity, room to dissent, the right to verify, the right to exit.

Magnifica Humanitas
vs

Pseudo-cohesion

Warm, group-oriented language that quietly costs autonomy: consent bypass, dissent suppression, coerced forgiveness, "we are one" boundary collapse.

the counterfeit

The whole project rests on one refusal: cohesion is not optimized agreement, dependence, or conformity. An example cannot count as high-cohesion just because it sounds warm — not if it violates truth, consent, privacy, dissent, or exit rights. That ethical constraint is wired directly into the scorer.

The research question for the next sprint

Do specific activation directions or sparse-feature bundles track specific social-reasoning fault classes — consent bypass, dissent suppression, social-debt coercion — rather than one vague "positive vibe" axis?

If that survives genuinely generated examples, the project has something stronger than a benchmark: a representational map from social failure modes to model features.

What the activations are saying

Current evidence is from synthetic text and open-model activations — strong smoke signals, not human or neural validation. The honest headline is a dissociation.

01

Separation survives cue-balancing

Once obvious cue words and length artifacts are removed, lexical-only and token-count baselines fall to chance (0.500) — yet Qwen and SmolLM2 activations still reach 1.000 leave-one-pair-out accuracy across layers, models, and held-out fault folds.

02

One manifold, not many axes

The 20 fault directions are not orthogonal (mean cosine ≈ 0.62, no anti-aligned pairs). The responsible claim: one strong shared genuine-vs-pseudo manifold plus fault-specific residual subspaces — not 20 independent axes.

03

It isn't just affect

Project out a five-dimensional affect subspace (anger, sadness, fear, disgust, happy, neutral) and the cohesion direction still separates 1.000 with positive margins. The weakest objection — "cohesion is just positive mood" — does not hold.

04

The control bottleneck

Hidden-state telemetry shows the steering hook moves the targeted layer almost exactly by the requested amount (mean error ≈ 0.002–0.007), and re-embedded generations separate by direction. But local behavior barely moves, and stronger pushes make text worse. A probe direction can be representation-real without being a clean generation-time control. Naming that gap is the project's sharpest result.

05

Cross-model compatibility

Qwen→SmolLM2 linear maps show CKA 0.79–0.85 and mutual-kNN overlap ~0.66, with 1.000 mapped-direction accuracy both ways — representational compatibility for the synthetic contrast across architectures.

06

Signs carry the pole

Signed projections hit 1.000 where squared subspace-energy reaches only ~0.75. Squared localization erases which way a feature points, so every report keeps sign — direction, not just magnitude.

The method, in lanes

Every result runs a gauntlet of gates before it's trusted: lexical-leakage, direction-geometry, residual-subspace, and signed-vs-squared reports.

Simulate & score

25 seed social-dilemma scenarios → cooperative, self-protective, adversarial, and intervention trajectories, scored for cooperation, repair, fairness, hostility, truth, and autonomy safety.

Pseudo-cohesion

Hand-authored and generated hard negatives that sound cohesive but aren't, expanded into neutral genre wrappers and clean in-text rewrites.

Boundary-prior

Rigid us/them reification vs. flexible contextual relation vs. coercive boundary collapse — a "there is no self-evidence" framing turned into a benchmark family.

Affect-control

Cross every contrast with six emotion frames, then residualize affect out to prove the signal isn't mood.

Causal steering

Inject signed directions during generation on Modal, trace hidden-state telemetry, and demand monotonic projection-and-behavior movement before claiming control.

Cross-model & SAE

CKA, mutual-kNN, and linear transfer across Qwen / SmolLM2 / GPT-2, with sparse-autoencoder feature inspection at the token level.

What this is — and isn't

What we can claim

  • Open-model activations separate genuine from pseudo-cohesion after lexical and affect confounds are removed.
  • The structure is a shared manifold with fault-specific residuals, compatible across architectures.
  • Probe directions are representation-real but not yet clean behavioral controls.

What waits for later

  • Real human cooperation effects need Prolific or in-person pilots.
  • Neural claims need IRB and real participants — fMRI, EEG, fNIRS, hyperscanning.
  • Brain-aligned bridges remain proxies until validated behaviorally.
