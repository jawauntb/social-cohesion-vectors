Engagement Trainer

Upload a CSV of your videos with their engagement metrics, train a predictor in your browser, then score future videos before you post. Everything runs locally — your data never leaves this page. This is the metadata/multimodal baseline; the TRIBE brain-trajectory features live in the repo pipeline.

1Upload training CSV

One row per video. Include numeric feature columns (duration, followers, topic scores, hook length, …) and engagement target columns (likes, comments, shares, saves, retention, …). A header row is required.

2Pick targets & features

Targets = what you want to predict. Features = what the model learns from. We auto-guessed based on column names — adjust as needed. Non-numeric columns are ignored.

3Training results

Ridge regression per target, with 5-fold cross-validated R² (how well it predicts videos it did not train on). R² near 1 is strong; near 0 means no better than guessing the average; negative means worse.

Model is also kept in this browser for scoring below.
4Score future videos

Two ways: enter one video's features, or upload a CSV of upcoming videos to score them in bulk.

5Score a video with the brain pipeline (TRIBE)

Send a video to the deployed TRIBE brain-response analyzer (Modal + Hugging Face, from the repo's video_analyzer function). It estimates the viewer's cognitive trajectory and returns predicted engagement + editor notes. Set your endpoint once — it's remembered in this browser.
